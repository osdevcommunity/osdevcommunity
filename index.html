About us

Welcome to our tech community!

We aim to create a platform where students can share notes and study materials with each other. We have a wealth of resources available, including job opportunities and internships. Our community is supported by mentors and admins to help students with work, provide referrals based on performance, hold events, and have working professionals working in top companies like Microsoft, Google, Wells Fargo, etc.

Besides, we are passionate about open source. We have been a community partner for several open source program and encouraged members to contribute to open source projects. We have also conducted hacktoberfest webinar to help members get started with open source journey.

We have collaborated with many other ed-tech giants like Scaler, Coding Ninjas, Newton school in which our members have participated in a huge number. We have also collaborated with GDG Indore for Devfest Indore. We were thrilled to collaborate with Intel and Hack2Skill for their event Gen AI hackthon using Intel one api.
